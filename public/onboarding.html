Step 5 — You're almost live
Your personalised setup
One step on your phone and Aida starts capturing every call automatically.

1 Activate call forwarding

Your phone keeps ringing as normal. Aida only picks up calls you miss — no answer, phone off, or you're already on another call. Tap each code below on this phone to turn that on (three separate codes, one per situation):

No answer
**61*+61344224989**15#
Phone off / unreachable
**62*+61344224989#
Busy / already on a call
**67*+61344224989#
  • Your number stays the same — callers dial you as normal, and answered calls never touch Aida
  • Only missed calls get forwarded to Aida's voicemail, which becomes a transcribed lead automatically
  • Tap each code above to dial it directly (it'll show a short confirmation from your carrier)
  • To turn a code off later, dial: ##61#, ##62#, ##67#
  • These codes work on Telstra, Optus and Vodafone postpaid mobiles. Some prepaid or MVNO plans use different codes — if a code doesn't confirm, let us know your carrier and we'll find the right one.

2 Connect your email

  • Go to your Aida dashboard → Connections
  • Click Set up Gmail / Outlook and sign in
  • Aida will draft follow-up emails after every call — you review and send

3 Make a test call

  • Ask someone to call your mobile number from a different phone
  • Don't answer it — let it ring out (or switch to flight mode first to test the unreachable code)
  • You should hear your Aida greeting, then be able to leave a message
  • Within 2 minutes, check your Aida dashboard — the call will appear with a transcript, summary, and draft email
